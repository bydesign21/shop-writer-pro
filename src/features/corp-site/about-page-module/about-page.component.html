About Us

Born from the heart of a bustling body shop, Shopwriterpro was created to provide the ultimate solution to an industry-wide dilemma - the absence of an in-house writer. We recognized the unjust loss faced by shop owners due to the lack of one crucial resource, an issue we passionately sought to rectify. Acknowledging the intricate dance of negotiation and appraisal with insurance companies, we believed in the necessity of a tool to guide body shops in showcasing the correct repair protocol. Our estimators, boasting a diverse background in Body Shops across the United States, share a unified mission.

Our Values

At Shopwriterpro, we are driven by a relentless pursuit of precision. We appreciate the crucial nature of meticulous estimates, and champion the use of Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) procedures, which form the backbone of safe and correct repair techniques. Presenting an exact estimate to the insurance firm or customer provides a clear roadmap to restoring the vehicle to its OEM specifications. We hold dear the principles of safety, accuracy, and shop owners' tranquility, constantly pushing boundaries to achieve these ideals.

Our Technology

Technological innovation sits at the core of Shopwriterpro. Our team of estimators harness the power of the state-of-the-art CCC estimating technology, coupled with industry-leading software, to generate precise estimates or necessary supplements. The breadth of access our estimators enjoy, to OEM procedures tailored for each make and model they are estimating, sets us apart. To further elevate the accuracy, we employ cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence technology. This powerful tool, functioning as a second set of eyes, efficiently detects damages through photographs, ensuring that every bit of accident-related damage is taken into account.
