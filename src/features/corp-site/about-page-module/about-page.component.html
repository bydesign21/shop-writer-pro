About Us

ShopWriterPro started in a Body Shop, looking for the answer to not having an in-house writer. There needs to be a better solution to this problem for shop owners, they don’t deserve to lose out on a significant amount of money just because of missing one employee. Even more importantly, Insurance companies can be difficult to negotiate and appraise damages, so we felt there should be something to help the body shop show the proper way to repair the vehicle. All of our estimators have experience working in Body Shops throughout the United States, and all have the same goal in mind.

Our Values

We value the importance of writing accurate estimates, with OEM procedures to show the safe and proper techniques to repair the vehicle. Giving an accurate estimate to the Insurance/customer shows an accurate game plan to repair the vehicle back to its OEM specifications. Safety, Accuracy, and the Body Shop owner’s peace of mind are what we value and strive to achieve. ￼

Our Technology

ShopWriterPro and our Estimators use CCC estimating technology, and industry leading software to write the estimate and/or supplement needed. Each Estimator also has access to OEM procedures for each make and model they are estimating. AI technology is used to detect the damage through photos, giving our estimators a second set of eyes when searching for all of the accident-related damages.
